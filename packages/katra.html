Explore the Beauty of Katra and enjoy

Katra serves as the starting point for the pilgrimage to the Vaishno Devi Temple, dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi, a manifestation of the Hindu goddess Durga. The temple is situated at an altitude of approximately 5,200 feet (1,580 meters) in the Trikuta Mountains and is one of the most visited religious sites in India.

. Pilgrims from across the country and around the world undertake the journey to the shrine, often covering a distance of about 13 kilometers (8 miles) on foot. The pilgrimage involves a steep trek from Katra to the temple, passing through scenic mountain trails, lush forests, and several smaller temples.

The journey is considered a significant spiritual experience and is believed to be an opportunity for personal reflection and devotion.
