Explore the Beauty of Leh and enjoy

Leh, the largest town and the administrative capital of the Ladakh Union Territory in northern India, is a mesmerizing destination nestled in the high-altitude desert of the Himalayas. Known for its stunning landscapes, unique culture, and serene environment, Leh offers a remarkable experience for travelers seeking adventure, spirituality, and natural beauty.

Leh is situated at an altitude of approximately 3,500 meters (11,500 feet) above sea level, surrounded by towering mountains and dramatic landscapes. The town is located in the Indus River Valley and is characterized by its barren, yet breathtaking, terrain.

The climate in Leh is stark and varied; winters are harsh with heavy snowfall and freezing temperatures, while summers are pleasantly mild and ideal for exploration.
