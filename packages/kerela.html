Explore the Beauty of kerela and enjoy

Kerala is a stunning state located on the southwestern coast of India, known for its lush landscapes, serene backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. This coastal paradise features beautiful beaches, rolling hills, and expansive spice and tea plantations, all set against the backdrop of the Western Ghats.

With a tropical climate, Kerala experiences humid summers and a monsoon season. The best time to visit is from November to February, when the weather is ideal for exploration.

The state is also famous for its vibrant festivals, traditional dance forms like Kathakali, and delicious cuisine featuring fresh seafood and spices. With its blend of natural beauty and cultural richness, Kerala is a must-visit destination for travelers.

Itenary

  • On arrival at the Airport/Railway Station in Cochin, you will be met and transfer to hotel.
  • After lunch go across the Cochin Harbor, which takes you to the gateway of the Arabian Sea.
  • On arrival at Fort Cochin, proceed on a sightseeing tour, which includes a visit to the Dutch Palace, Jewish Synagogue, St.Francis Church, Chinese Fishing Nets.
  • Rest of the day at leisure
  • Overnight stay at Cochin.
  • Morning after breakfast embark on fascinating journey to Munnar enjoying the stunning panoramic view of the Cardamom hills green Carpets of tea plantation enroute.
  • On arrival, overnight stay at your Hotel..
  • A morning sightseeing trip of Munnar, visiting the Eravikulam National park where you can see the very rare mountain goats locally called the Nilgiry Thar, Mattupetty Dam, Old Munnar Town and Local market.
  • Rest of the day at leisure and enjoy another over night stay at your Hotel.
  • After an early breakfast, check out from the hotel & proceed to the Wild Life Sanctuary at Periyar/Thekkady and check in at the Hotel/Resort.
  • Go on an evening game viewing cruise on the Periyar Lake.
  • .Here you will be able to see a large variety of wildlife, which include elephants, monkeys, deer, Wild boar etc, and quite a lot of unique birds can also be spotted.
  • Later, come back to the hotel, relax & enjoy stay overnight.
  • Early morning drive down to Alleppey; view the scenic picturesque landscapes on your way.
  • Check in at the Hotel/Resort on the bank of the Vembanad Lake.
  • Spend the evening at leisure and stay overnight at the Resort.
  • Depart as per schedule to Airport for your onward journey.
